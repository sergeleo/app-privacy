Privacy Policy for My Food Diary

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

My Food Diary is a private food journaling app for saving daily meal photos and notes.

Information We Collect

My Food Diary lets you save photos, notes, dates, and optional location data for your food memories. Location data is only stored when you allow location access on your device.

How Your Information Is Stored

Your diary data is stored on your device. When iCloud is available, the app may store your diary data in your personal iCloud Documents storage so it can be available across your Apple devices. If iCloud is not available, the app stores data locally on your device.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide the app’s diary features, including showing your saved photos, notes, dates, and future location-based features such as a food map.

Data Sharing

My Food Diary does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The app does not include third-party advertising or third-party analytics.

Location Data

If you grant location permission, the app may save the approximate location where a food memory was created. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings. Food memories can still be saved without location data.

Photos

Photos you add to My Food Diary are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud storage when iCloud is available. The app does not upload photos to any separate server operated by us.

Children’s Privacy

My Food Diary is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.
